Our Story

35 Years of
Feeding Chennai

Sri Murugan Mess was started in 1988 by Krishnaswamy and his wife Meenakshi on Usman Road, T. Nagar. What began as a modest 8-table shop feeding workers has grown into one of Chennai's most beloved family restaurants.

We source vegetables daily from Koyambedu market. Our sambar recipe has not changed in 35 years. Every batter is stone-ground in-house. No MSG, no artificial flavours, no food colour — only the real spices your grandparents loved.

Today, Krishnaswamy's son Karthik runs the kitchen, carrying forward every recipe exactly as it was taught to him.

100% vegetarian kitchen — no meat, fish, or eggs, ever
Fresh batter ground daily — no ready-made mixes
Filtered water used in all cooking and beverages
FSSAI certified & hygiene audited every 6 months
